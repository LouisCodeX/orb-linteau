FAQ

Most common questions before starting a project.

How do you open a load‑bearing wall safely?

We start with a structural assessment, then size the beam (IPN/HEB) and secure the work area. Goal: a clean opening with proper supports and neat repairs.

How much does a concrete slab cost?

It depends on surface, thickness, prep (groundworks, rebar), and site access. We provide a clear quote with options if needed.

How long does underpinning work take?

Depending on complexity and scope, it can range from a few weeks to several months. We phase the work to keep the structure safe.
